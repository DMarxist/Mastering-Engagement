Mastering Engagement in Care Home Quality Improvement

A Toolkit for Healthcare Staff

The Engagement Imperative

Genuine engagement is the cornerstone of successful and sustainable improvements in care quality, resident experience, and staff well-being.

Defining Meaningful Engagement

Engagement must be tailored to the distinct roles, needs, and capacities of each group, moving beyond token consultation to active, authentic participation.

👥

For Staff

Active contribution in identifying problems, co-designing solutions, and owning the improvement process.

👵

For Residents

Ensuring their preferences, values, and "What Matters To You?" are central to all decisions impacting their lives.

👨‍👩‍👧

For Families

Recognizing them as indispensable partners, leveraging their intimate knowledge and insights for holistic solutions.

The Heart of the Matter: Engaging Residents & Families

All QI efforts must be grounded in what genuinely matters to those receiving care. This means uncovering their core needs and actively involving them in co-designing solutions.

Experience-Based Co-Design (EBCD)

EBCD is a powerful methodology for building solutions with residents, families, and staff, not for them.

1. Gather Experiences

Collect stories from staff, residents & families.

↓

2. Identify Priorities

3. Joint Co-Design
